FAQs

Logging In

I'm getting a system unavailable message when I log in. How can I fix it?

Try clearing your web browser's cookies and cache, then restart.  

Should I use my PIN or password for online banking?

Members should create their own secure online banking passwords to prevent unauthorized account access. Passwords also work for mobile banking, Card Guard, Quicken and QuickBooks.

What are the password requirements?

They must be eight to 32 characters long and must contain at least one letter and one numeral.

What if I forgot my password?

Go to the PIN/Password page, then click the Forgot My Password link to change it. (Please note you’ll need answers to all three challenge questions. If you don't know the answers, call 1-800-999-3961 and we'll re-set the password.)

When logging in, can I use my PIN instead of a password?

A secure password is required for online & mobile banking, Card Guard, Quicken and QuickBooks. Use your PIN to make ATM withdrawals, debit purchases, free telephone banking transaction and member service calls.

Organization

How can I categorize transactions?

This is available exclusively through Money Manager. You’ll get consistency with both mobile & online banking when you’re creating budgets, tracking expenses, and making additional plans.

How do I review online statements?

Click Statements in the Quick Links section, then use the drop-down menu to select the period you want. Hit View Statement and it will automatically download the PDF file. Your computer must have Adobe Reader to work.

How long are my online statements available?

The last two years' worth.

Transfers

Why don't I see a cancel link next to my transfer?

Certain transfers, such as those on loan repayment agreements, can’t be edited with online banking. Please call 1-800-999-3961 to modify transfers like these.

How do I delete a pending transfer?

On the Transfers tab, scroll down for pending transactions. Click the red X icon next to the transfer you’re deleting.

How do I change recurring transfers?

Go to the Transfers tab and scroll down to see pending transactions. Click the edit icon next to the transfer you’re modifying.

Personalization

Can you send alerts to a cell phone?

Yes. Alerts are available via our free mobile banking app. To get them on a cell phone that is smart capable, follow the instructions on this page.

How can I personalize my account names?

Click the Settings tab and select Accounts. Then choose the account you want.

How do I update my personal information?

Click the Settings tab, then choose the Profile section.

How does account linking work?

With this feature, you can access secondary and joint accounts through a single login. Also available are primary owner, joint-owner, and co-signer accounts.

How do I personalize my America First Visa® cards?

To customize debit & credit cards with free Picture Perfect, select the Settings tab, then choose Personalize Your Visa Debit/Credit Card in the Additional Services box.

Security

Can I cancel my PIN or cards if they've been compromised, lost, or stolen?

Yes. To cancel your PIN or report a lost or stolen card, click on the Settings tab, select Security and Alerts, then choose Lost/Stolen PIN or Card.

How do I change my password?

Click the Settings tab at the top of the online banking homepage. Then select the Profile box.

Quicken

I can’t use Quicken with Express Connect. What can I do?

If Express Connect isn’t working, we recommend changing to Direct Connect. Just follow the steps below.

  • Go to Tools and click Account Lists
  • Choose the account with the problem and click Edit Account
  • Select on Online Services
  • Click on the bottom blue link: Improve your Connection through Direct Connect
  • You’ll be asked for a user ID and password
  • Enter your account number as the user
  • Enter your PIN for the password
  • Click Next
  • All your accounts will show
  • Go to the drop-down menu and change Add to Link
  • Find your account from the list
  • Repeat for each account until all read Link, followed by the account name (e.g., Link-Checking)
  • Click Next
  • Your accounts will be updated
  • Click on Finish

After that, you’ll use Quicken to modify any account information.
